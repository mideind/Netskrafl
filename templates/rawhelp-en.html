Welcome to Explo!

Explo is a crossword game where you match your vocabulary and word-building skills against other humans or robot players. The rules are similar to those of classic crossword board games.

At the start of a game, you have seven letter tiles that are shown in the rack below the board. If it's your turn — indicated by a flag next to your username — you can play the tiles by dragging them with the mouse: Click on a tile, drag it to the intended square and release the mouse button. You can also use a finger to drag tiles on touch screens, such as tablets and smartphones. You can move tiles freely between squares or back to the rack if you have second thoughts. The Recall button and the Esc key on the keyboard send all tiles from the board back to the rack, and the Shuffle button and the Backspace key randomly shuffle the rack.

When you have placed one or more tiles as you like, click the button to submit the move. It's now your opponent's turn (as indicated by the flag). She replies with her move and then it's again your turn. Robot players always respond immediately. Fresh tiles laid down by your opponent are shown on the board in a red color.

If you can't or don't want to lay down any tiles, click the Pass button. You get no points for a pass, and it becomes your opponent's turn to move.

If you want to exchange tiles from the rack into the tile bag, click the Exchange button. You pick the tiles you want to exchange by clicking on them. When the exchange is completed you get no points and it's your opponent's turn. When fewer than seven tiles are left in the bag, an exchange is no longer allowed.

A game is completed when no tiles remain in the bag, or by a "stalemate" where both players have passed three times in a row (six times in total). You can also resign from a game by clicking the Resign button. In that case, you lose all your points for the game as well as the game itself.

Frá og með október 2015 notar Netskrafl nýjan íslenskan skraflpoka sem búinn var til á vegum Skraflfélags Íslands, til viðbótar þeim eldri og upprunalega.

Á eldri (upphaflega) skraflpokanum eru ýmsir vankantar, eins og skraflarar kannast við. Í honum eru til dæmis of mörg E sem gefa of fá stig; stafirnir Ú og Ý eru happafengur enda stigaháir en auðvelt að koma þeim út, og svo mætti áfram telja.

Við hönnun nýs skraflpoka var leitast við að gera alla stafi því sem næst jafn ákjósanlega, í þeim skilningi að þeir staldri jafn lengi við í rekka leikmanns að meðaltali. Leitað var að bestu lausn með því að láta tölvu prófa sig áfram í átt að betri poka í yfir tíu milljónum viðureigna. Þess var jafnframt gætt að hæfilegt jafnvægi væri milli sérhljóða og samhljóða, og að dreifing stafanna væri nálægt því sem gerist í íslenskum texta almennt.

Með nýja pokanum er unnt að leggja niður lengri orð að meðaltali, og heppni í stafadrætti skiptir eilítið minna máli en áður. Í honum eru 100 flísar í stað 104 í eldri pokanum, en samanlögð stig flísanna eru jafnmörg.

Gamli pokinn er nú aðeins notaður í viðureign ef hvorugur leikmaður er merktur með nýja pokanum. Þjarkarnir laga sig ætíð að ósk mennska leikmannsins.

Tilkoma nýja pokans hefur engin áhrif á Elo-stig leikmanna eða útreikning þeirra.

Explo accepts two letter words from the relevant English dictionary, usually OTCWL2014 (for U.S. English) or SOWPODS (for other English-speaking countries and regions).

You can access the word list during a game by clicking on the tab marked with a life preserver .
